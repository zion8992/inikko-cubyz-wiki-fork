# FAQ
There have been a lot of questions recently about how things work in the game. Here is a non-exhaustive list of the most commonly asked ones.

# Gameplay Questions 
## How do I play the game?
Go to [Cubyz Releases](https://github.com/PixelGuys/Cubyz/releases/latest) page and download the release build for your operating system. You can find the Installation Guide [here](/installation.html).

## Game doesn't start, what do I do?
Join Cubyz Discord server and look through questions in the [Help Section](https://discord.com/channels/443805812390100992/1183393935331176458).

## How do I heal? 
Die. No seriously. There is currently no means to heal.

## How do I eat food? 
Currently you don't. The stomach bar is purely cosmetic.

## Why is my game lagging? 
Try turning your "LOD Distance" down. You will only see a difference in details of distant chunks.

## How deep/wide is Cubyz? 
2 billion blocks on each axis (although the game works until around 10 billion blocks). For all regular gameplay purposes, it's practically infinite!

# Development Questions 

## How do I install an addon? 
Take a look at the [Addon Installation Guide](development/addons/installation.html).

## How do I make an addon? 
Take a look at the [Addon Creation Guide](development/addons/index.html).

## Can X feature be added to the game? 
Wow! X feature sounds really cool! See if it follows the [Game Design and Principles](https://github.com/PixelGuys/Cubyz/blob/0.0.0/docs/GAME_DESIGN_PRINCIPLES.md) and try implementing it!

## I really want to contribute, where do I start? 
Cubyz relies heavily on community support, and there are many ways to help! You can check out our dedicated [How to Contribute](development/how_to_contribute.md) page for full details. 

* **For Code & Addons:** Read the [Contribution Guidelines](https://github.com/PixelGuys/Cubyz/blob/master/docs/CONTRIBUTING.md) and look for open issues on GitHub.
* **For Community Roles:** You can apply to join our YouTube, Discord, Wiki, GitHub, or Reddit teams.

If you have any questions or just want to chat with the team, feel free to ask in our [Discord](https://discord.gg/8HhcnDUV).
