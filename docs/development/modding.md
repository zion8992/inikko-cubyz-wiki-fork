## Official Modding

The official compile-time modding API has not been finished. You can watch its progress in [#1528](https://github.com/PixelGuys/Cubyz/issues/1528) and see the initial discussions in [#1507](https://github.com/PixelGuys/Cubyz/issues/1507).

## Unofficial Modding

Here are some forks of Cubyz with custom modding support, but they may not be up-to-date you can find these below:

* [Web Assembly (wasm) modding](https://addons.ashframe.net/addon.html?id=24)
* [Compile time modding](https://github.com/KewaiiGamer/ElevatorMod-Cubyz/tree/main)
* [Cubyz lua integration](https://github.com/LinaPlusPlus/Cubyz/tree/integrate_lua)
